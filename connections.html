The Connections

There will be a new connections puzzle every week. Make four groups of four using the sixteen words. Each group's words will all be connected in some way. For example, the words SPRUCE, ASPEN, POPLAR, and PINE would be linked since they all refer to kinds of trees. Beware, since certain words can be misleading! The solution will be revealed next week.
